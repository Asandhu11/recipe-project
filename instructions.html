How to Follow Our Recipes

Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy, then set aside.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss spaghetti with pancetta, then mix in egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
Cooking Tips

For a creamier texture, reserve a bit of pasta water and mix it in before serving.
